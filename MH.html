Missionary Homecomings

What does a missionary homecoming video entail

An LDS missionary homecoming video or montage is a heartfelt compilation that captures the joy, gratitude, and celebration surrounding a missionary’s return home. It typically includes clips of the missionary’s arrival at the airport, emotional reunions with family, moments of welcome at home, and sometimes footage or photos from their mission to highlight the journey they’ve completed. Set to meaningful music, the montage aims to reflect the missionary’s growth, testimony, and experiences while also preserving the excitement of the homecoming day. It becomes a treasured keepsake for the missionary and their loved ones, allowing them to relive the emotions and memories of that special moment whenever they watch it.
