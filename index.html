About Me

Hey! I'm Jade, an energetic and detail‑obsessed videographer who thrives on capturing emotional, cinematic moments. Over the last several years, I’ve honed my craft filming weddings, love stories, and joyful reunions. My work blends modern editing, warm tones, and storytelling that feels authentic and personal.

My videos have been featured in local bridal magazines, shared widely on social media, and cherished by dozens of happy couples and families. My goal? To freeze moments in time—beautifully and intentionally.
